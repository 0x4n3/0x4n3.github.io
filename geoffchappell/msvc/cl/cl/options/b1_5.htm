CL /B1_5

This option names the in-between C/C++ compiler.

Syntax

/B1_5pathname

The required argument consists of whatever follows immediately within the same command-line token, else is the whole of the next command-line token.

Behaviour

Whatever is named by pathname becomes the in-between compiler module, which is ordinarily not defined.
